About

Hi, I'm Marc Weingärtner,

my love for game development came from creating small personal Skyrim mods. This and my passion for computer science were the main reasons why I enrolled at the University of Würzburg to study Games Engineering.

Uni Projects

The first projects i was tasked to develop are "Bonk!" and "CCTB - Spatial Agents". Both were partner projects. Although the latter required us to work in cooperation with a stakeholder who determined the scope of the project. The development of "Brambleborn" was my first big group asignment with about 20 people where each participant was given a role they had to fulfill. For all these projects i had to learn C# and how to deal with Unity and Godot engines, as well as git.

Game Jams

Sometimes my friends and i gather to participate in one of the local Franken Game Jams so we can create yet another fun and chaotic game. "Kallaxmaker" and "Child Delivery Service" taught me to work quickly and efficient in small teams of up to 6 people.

Custom Engine

For the most recent solo project I am working on, the task is to create a game using only C/C++ and some permitted libraries, for exmaple OpenGL. I decided to build a custom Engine with an underlying Entity-Component-System (ECS) in order to develop "RotZone: Crimson Outbreak", a first person movement shooter.
